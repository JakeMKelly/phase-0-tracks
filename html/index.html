"Farming"

"Farming is an essential part of every society. It provides food for all of the members of said society and can be a basis for its economy.

Here are some things needed for farming:

  • Good soil
  • Plenty of water
  • Seeds

Here are some steps to grow crops:

  1. Till the soil
  2. Plant the seeds
  3. Water regularly
  4. Harvest when ready
